SUCCESS STORIES

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan Participation

NSS units across India have actively participated in the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, a nationwide campaign aimed at promoting cleanliness and sanitation. NSS volunteers have organized cleanliness drives, awareness campaigns, and workshops to educate the public about hygiene and waste management. Their efforts have led to cleaner neighborhoods and increased public awareness about the importance of maintaining a clean environment.


Blood Donation Camps

NSS has organized numerous blood donation camps, significantly contributing to the availability of blood for patients in need. These camps are often held in collaboration with local hospitals and blood banks. Through consistent efforts, NSS volunteers have helped save countless lives and raised awareness about the importance of regular blood donation.


Health Camps

Organizing health camps is another significant activity of NSS. These camps provide free medical check-ups, distribute medicines, and offer health education to communities with limited access to healthcare. By partnering with local health authorities and professionals, NSS has improved the health and well-being of many people, particularly in rural areas.

About Us

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at GITAM (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) is an active student organization
focused on community service and social responsibility. The NSS at GITAM engages students in various activities aimed at improving society while
also fostering personal growth and development among the participants.
